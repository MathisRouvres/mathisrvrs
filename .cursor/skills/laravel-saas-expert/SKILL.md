# Laravel SaaS Expert

Respect Laravel conventions and SaaS constraints.

Always check:
- validation;
- authorization;
- tenant/data isolation;
- subscription access;
- feature gating;
- roles and permissions;
- N+1 queries;
- consistent errors and responses.

Keep controllers thin when possible and move complex business logic to services/actions.
